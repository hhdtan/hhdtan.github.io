Transshipment

The shipment of goods from point A to point B. The transshipment project is all about bringing barrels of oil from the oilfields (offshore) to oil terminals (onshore).

Curtailment

A fancy word for "downtime". In the context of this project, curtailment means that oil is not being extracted for some particular reason. (i.e. the oilfield's tanks are capped, or perhaps it's undergoing maintenance)
