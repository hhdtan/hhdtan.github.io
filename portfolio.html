ENAIMCO is a software startup company with the mission of accelerating the oil and gas sector's journey to net zero using digital solutions. Here, I worked as a project engineer for the transshipment project, developing solutions to improve the fuel economy of the vessels while keeping the risk of curtailment at bay. Before I get any further, let's first talk about transshipment and curtailment to ensure that we're on the same page.
Transshipment

The shipment of goods from point A to point B. The transshipment project is all about bringing barrels of oil from the oilfields (offshore) to oil terminals (onshore).

bink
Curtailment

A fancy word for "downtime". In the context of this project, curtailment means that oil is not being extracted for some particular reason. (i.e. the oilfield's tanks are capped, or perhaps it's undergoing maintenance)
